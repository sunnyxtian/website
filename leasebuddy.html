Lease Buddy

Figma , Fall 2023

INFO 200, "Foundations of Informatics" @ UW.

The Brief

Students were asked to create an information system that solved any real world issue. I worked with 4 other students to prototype Lease Buddy, an app that tries to help international students at UW get housing.

Problem

Our group included 2 international students from China. They shared about how int'l students often travel back home during the summer, which creates a marketplace for subleases. However, int'l students often communicate using platforms like WeChat that non-int'l students don't use. In my group of 5, 4 of us knew how to speak Mandarin.
